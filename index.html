About

Learn More About Us

This is the best centre inside Dhaka city where All marriages, the other program can be done following all the rituals as per their religion in one place.

With one of the largest convention center, Mirpur DOHS will stay at the forefront of the ceremonial proceedings and will become the most preferred destination for your personal and family ceremonial events.

  • It can accommodate 350 persons at a time in dining position.
  • Only place where you can through your event/program from 50 to 850 people.
  • The hall is 9000 sft close space with additional 3000 sft open terrace which will give you an elegant, exclusive event for your nearer and dearer!!

The hall is available to the members of Freedom Fighters, Armed forces, Cadet College, resident of Mirpur DOHS at subsidy and to the general people at normal rate. It is supported by a modern kitchen and served by a set of highly skilled and professional catering staffs.

We do arrangements for Official parties, Gaye Holud , Wedding Ceremony , Wedding Reception , Baby Shower , Birthday Party and many more . From a family dinner, hang-out with friends, to a romantic date with your partner, can be made much more special with the mouth watering dishes prepared by our selected chefs. We are very strict and rigid for hygiene of our customers. We work best to maintain our quality. Your satisfaction is our main concern.
